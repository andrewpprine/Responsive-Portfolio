About Me

I strive to utilize my experience and expertise in media, education, and technology to create and refine informational tools for a global audience.

As a teacher, I collaborate with multiple resources and teams across a variety of fields to design, implement, and evaluate dynamic plans, programs, and activities for a range of clients. As a photographer, I possess critical observation skills, an impeccable attention to detail, and a keen analysis for client habits and interests. As a journalist, I am personally dedicated to tracking developments across a wide range of topics, utilizing extensive research opportunities to maintain sound awareness in an era of rapid information evolution, and relating information in the most grammatically accurate manner possible. And on a personal level, I am an avid devotee for acquiring information, interacting with technology, and driven to provide others with the tools to realize capabilities past, present, and future.
